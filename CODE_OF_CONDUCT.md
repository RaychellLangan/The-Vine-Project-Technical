Code of Conduct
The Vine Project is experimental research exploring geometric approaches to AI architecture. We welcome thoughtful technical discussion.
Technical Discussion
We encourage critical engagement with:

Mathematical formulation
Architectural design
Measurable system behavior

Please keep discussions grounded in observable, computable phenomena.
Confidentiality
Do not share private correspondence or derivative work without explicit permission from the maintainer.
Maintenance
This project is maintained by an individual researcher. Responses to issues and contributions may take time. Thank you for your patience and thoughtful participation.
Report violations through GitHub's reporting system.
